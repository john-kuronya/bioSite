Welcome to my bioSite!

Welcome! My name is Jennifer Kuronya, and I’m proud of the journey that has shaped my career and passion for helping others. For 25 years, I served as a parole agent with the State of Pennsylvania’s Parole Board, balancing public safety with supporting individuals in their reintegration journeys. After retiring from that role, I transitioned into education and have spent the past four years as a paraprofessional in a kindergarten classroom at Executive Education Charter School in Allentown, PA.

Both careers have allowed me to make meaningful contributions to my community, whether guiding individuals toward new opportunities or fostering young learners’ growth. I’m passionate about making a difference and excited to share my story with you!
